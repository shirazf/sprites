LET'S GET MARRIED

Let's Get Married is a realistic love game. Players complete various tasks assigned to them by NPCs in hopes that these tasks will lead them to a lover. They may have to fight closeted ghosts, paint walls with blood, or earn money in order to find the person of their dreams. The player must get married in order to win the game.
